How to Play Ship Captain Crew

🎯

1. Get Ship-Captain-Crew

Roll to get 6 (Ship), 5 (Captain), and 4 (Crew) in that exact order. They'll auto-lock when you roll them!

📦

2. Maximize Cargo

Once you have Ship-Captain-Crew, the remaining 2 dice become your cargo score. Higher is better - can you get double 6s for Midnight?

🎉

3. Hit Midnight!

The ultimate goal: 6-5-4-6-6! When you hit Midnight, enjoy epic fireworks and bragging rights. It's the perfect score!
